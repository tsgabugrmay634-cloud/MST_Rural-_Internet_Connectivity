========================================
COMPLEXITY ANALYSIS
========================================
Kruskal's Algorithm Complexity:
  - Sorting edges: O(E log E)
  - Union-Find operations: O(E α(V))
  - Overall: O(E log E)

Where:
  V = Number of villages = 100
  E = Number of possible connections = 4950
  α = Inverse Ackermann function (practically constant)

Space Complexity: O(V + E)
  - Village array: O(V)
  - Edge array: O(E)
  - Union-Find structures: O(V)
